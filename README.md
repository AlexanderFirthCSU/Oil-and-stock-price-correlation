# Oil-and-stock-price-correlation
This is a personal project where I look at the correlation of the price of oil and major stock sector data in python using Yahoo Finance data


From what I have seen in the findings, there is a minor correlation between oil prices and stock prices, with the largest correlation being .29 with energy. As a result, I believe the price of a barrel of oil cannot be used to predict future changes in the stock market. Because of the multitude of variables, I believe the correlation between oil prices and stock prices is muddled. If I ever continue this project, I would try to account for these variables or examine more extreme price changes and how they impact the stock market in those instances (i.e., how a massive change in oil prices affects the stock market). Perhaps look at other economic indicators (GDP, Consumer spending, etc.) to see how oil prices affect them.
